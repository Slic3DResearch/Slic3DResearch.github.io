Our Development Journey

Current Focus

Phase 1: Core Tech Refinement & Validation

Optimizing microwave field control for uniformity, rigorously validating material compatibility (Alumina, Zirconia focus), and establishing robust process parameters for consistent, high-quality results. Building and testing functional prototypes.

Near Term

Phase 2: Beta Systems & Pilot Programs

Developing integrated beta systems for deployment and testing with key industry partners. Expanding material portfolio based on collaborative needs. Gathering critical real-world performance data and feedback.

Mid Term

Phase 3: Targeted Commercial Launch

Launching the first Slic3D commercial platform aimed at specific high-value industrial applications. Establishing manufacturing, distribution, and customer support infrastructure.

Long Term

Phase 4: Platform Expansion & Accessibility

Developing next-generation systems with potential variations in size, throughput, and cost. Exploring broader material classes (composites, non-oxides) and driving towards wider market accessibility, potentially including smaller-scale research units.
