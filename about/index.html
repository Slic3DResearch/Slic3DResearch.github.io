About Slic3D Research

Mission: Slic3D Research is relentless in its pursuit of transforming the landscape of additive manufacturing. By integrating diverse engineering disciplines, the organization seeks to expand capabilities and redefine standards. Its mission is not merely to innovate but to revolutionize the conception and production of objects, setting a bold new course for the future.

Vision: Envision a world where 3D printing unlocks the creation of objects previously deemed impossible. With a cross-disciplinary approach, Slic3D Research empowers engineers, hobbyists, and consumers alike to harness this platform, enabling them to design groundbreaking solutions that once resided only in the realm of imagination. Together, we will push the boundaries of possibility.

History: Established in 2024, Slic3D Research emerged from a profound passion for synthesizing engineering expertise across technological sectors to propel advancements forward. This dedication has culminated in the development of cutting-edge solutions poised to disrupt and transform industries, driving progress like never before.

Why? Slic3D Research embodies a vision—a manifestation of imagination brought to life through additive manufacturing. It stands as a testament to the belief in relentless innovation, inspiring future engineers to transcend traditional boundaries, forge interdisciplinary solutions, and cultivate growth. Join us in this journey to redefine what’s possible.

About me

My name is Stephen, and I founded Slic3D Research. I have spent my life to the pursuit of progress and founded Slic3D Research in order to share my results with those who might take the technology further toward the future.

My research in electromagnetic properties of 3D printed objects gave me the opportunity to expand across two distinct disciplines in engineering: aerospace and electromagnetics. As it turns out, further advancements could be realized by manipulating the distinct advantages garnered from additive processes in terms of electromagnetic performance.

Throughout my career, I have continuously pursued innovation and don't intend on stopping any time soon. The advancements I've made in additive manufacturing are a piece of the puzzle I'm trying to solve.

I want 3D printing to finally achieve the types of results that have been promised for years. From hobbyists toys, to medical prosthetics, to military applications, additive manufacturing has the capability modernize and grow in each of these industries.

Thank you for joining me on this adventure.
