Explore the Technology Why It Matters

The Ceramic Bottleneck

Advanced ceramics offer incredible properties—strength, heat resistance, biocompatibility—but traditional manufacturing is slow, energy-intensive, and geometrically limiting.

Lengthy Processing

Conventional kiln firing cycles take hours, sometimes days, creating significant production delays and limiting iteration speed.

High Energy Costs

Heating large, inefficient furnaces consumes vast amounts of energy, impacting both manufacturing cost and environmental sustainability.

Design Constraints

Slow, uniform heating restricts the complexity of achievable geometries and hinders the creation of functionally integrated ceramic parts.

The Slic3D Breakthrough

Microwave Sintering.
Perfected.

Our proprietary technology leverages precisely controlled microwave energy to sinter ceramic materials directly, rapidly, and efficiently, completely bypassing the limitations of conventional furnaces.

  • Sintering in minutes, not hours or days.
  • Significantly reduced energy usage per part.
  • Enabling complex internal structures and geometries.
