Our Approach

Interdisciplinary Synergy

Slic3D Research thrives at the intersection of disciplines. We believe true breakthroughs emerge when expertise in materials science, electromagnetics, mechanical engineering, software, and process control converge.

Our culture fosters relentless curiosity, rigorous experimentation, and a collaborative spirit focused on translating fundamental scientific discovery into tangible, impactful technological advancements.

Founder Spotlight

Stephen

Founder & CEO/CTO

With a background combining aerospace engineering and applied electromagnetics, Stephen identified the potential for microwave energy to revolutionize ceramic processing. Driven by a fascination with overcoming the limitations of traditional manufacturing, he founded Slic3D Research to develop and commercialize this vision.

"We're not just iterating on existing methods; we're building a fundamentally new approach to creating high-performance materials, enabling designs and applications previously out of reach."
